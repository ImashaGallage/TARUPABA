ABOUT



If ever a restaurant encapsulated the genteel aspirations of its target audience, it must be Silverspoon of Silverstream.A converted house, it is a cafe by day and restaurant by night, when starched white tablecloths are laid over the tables and napkins are ironed into fancy patterns.

There are all the trappings of contemporary good taste - the wooden floor, the wooden cafe chairs, the feature wall of patterned wallpaper. It's all very safe, if a tad unmemorable.Only one piece of artwork is to be seen, a photographic enlargement of a sterling silver spoon. One recent Wednesday night, local families of note and assorted fragments thereof gathered around this shrine.
